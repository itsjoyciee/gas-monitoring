Sulfur Dioxide (SO₂)

What is Sulfur Dioxide?

Sulfur dioxide is a toxic gas with a pungent, irritating smell. It's a major air pollutant and contributor to acid rain.

Sources

  • Burning of fossil fuels
  • Industrial processes
  • Volcanic eruptions
  • Metal smelting

Health Effects

  • Respiratory irritation
  • Coughing and wheezing
  • Throat and eye irritation
  • Breathing difficulties
  • Aggravation of asthma

Safety Thresholds

  • 0-0.5 ppm: Safe level
  • 0.5-2 ppm: Mild irritation
  • 2-5 ppm: Increased symptoms
  • 5+ ppm: Dangerous level
